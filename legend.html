THE DARKNESS WITHIN

Once upon a time, the fountain of Darkness coexisted with light to maintain the balance, the dimensions, and the world from collapsing. It was unknown to the people of light, whilst those on the other side thrived within it.

Until... another fountain had been summoned in the horizon.

To spawn a fountain is simple: You have to strike the ground with a sharp object as you channel your determination and will into it.

However, to maintain the balance of the two worlds, there should only be one existing Dark Fountain. Too many fountains existing at once will only cause destruction, death, and for Earth to take its last breath.

One must be left wondering: Who is the Knight who struck the ground?

THE PROPHECY

In the prophecy, the three heroes must consist of a human, a monster, and the Prince of the Dark, with the latter being Ralsei.

To close a fountain is to seal it with the human's soul, and to save the world is to seal all extra fountains within the vicinity... and ultimately banish the Angel's Heaven.
