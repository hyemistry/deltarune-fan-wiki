Normal Route

Weird Route

THE PROJECT

After school, the now-friends Kris and Susie encounters Noelle, which depending on the player's choices in Chapter 1, she will give Susie a box of chalk. She then invites the two to join them in the local Librarby to begin their school projects.

However, when they arrived in the library, something was amiss.

Yep. Another one of those pesky fountains.

A CYBER'S WORLD?

As the two entered the found dark world, what they see is beyond a spectacle—green crossroads with electronic skies.

However, they also found Noelle, who was later captured by Queen.

MARTLET'S BROTHER

Later down the road, they finally encountered Berdly...

on the enemy's side!

How convenient.

CYBER CITY

After their first encounter with Berdly, they were forced to traverse the area down below, which then led to a new area—the Cyber City.

NOELLE JOINS THE PARTY

After some time, the party of three decided to split up, with Susie forcing Ralsei to go with her since you always get to decide.

Later on, you find Noelle, who was hiding from Queen. Queen, who was hiding from Berdly, unfortunatelly arrives, offering you a truce. After some time, Berdly arrives, who also offered you a truce.

That's a lot honestly. But it gets better!

When both Berdly and Queen left, Noelle offers you a third truce, which then led to her joining the party.

NOT YOU AGAIN

Who would've known?

Berdly fights you and Noelle for a second time.

BUY NOW for [20 KROMER]!!

Though you and Noelle continued on your way, you guys encountered Queen once more, who didn't notice that she was talking to Noelle despite it being blatantly obvious.

After some time of traversing traffic, you arrive at a rather short predicament.

Spamton.

REUNITED

Later, you and Noelle reunite with Susie and Ralsei. Depending on your choices, you can get up to a maximum of 6 party members temporarily (including Berdly and The Original Starwalker!)

SHE ONLY PLAYS MOBILE GAMES

As soon as you, Susie, Ralsei and Noelle arrived in front of Queen's mansion, you encounter Berdly again. This time, Queen captures everyone but Ralsei (she only had 4 cages at the time).

Welp. That journey was fun.

THIS GUY? AGAIN?

Yep.

He can't be stopped. Even if you were in the middle of saving Noelle from Queen.

ATTACK OF THE KILLER QUEEN

As soon as the party was completed again, you three find...

Queen? And Berdly being controlled by those weird wires?

SHE HAS A FINAL FORM

If that wasn't a hard-enough boss fight, she has another.

And it's larger.

DARK FOUNTAIN SEALED

THIS WAS SUPPOSED TO BE A DAY FOR DOING THE SCHOOL PROJECT.

DARK FOUNTAIN... OPENED?

At their house in the middle of the night, hours after Kris came home from the library, they then ripped you out of their body, pierced the ground with their knife, and...

Opened a dark fountain in the living room.

SNOWGRAVE ROUTE

Remember when you made a truce with Noelle?

This is what happens when you force her to use her spell, IceShock, on every encounter.

FROZEN CHICKEN

And what happens when you force her to use SnowGrave on Berdly.

SECRET FINAL BOSS

You fight Spamton NEO alone in front of the Dark Fountain.
