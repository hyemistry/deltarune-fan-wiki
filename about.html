about deltArune

DELTARUNE is an indie episodic RPG game made by Toby Fox. It tackles the adventures of the three heroes, Kris, Susie, and Ralsei in an attempt to save the world from an impending eternal darkness caused by Dark Fountains.

DELTARUNE is the succeeding game to UNDERTALE, another game by Toby Fox released in 2015. Majority of the characters in UNDERTALE are featured in DELTARUNE as non-playable characters.
