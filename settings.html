CHAPTER 1

HOMETOWN

Hometown is a town situated in the Light World. It is the, well, hometown of the residents Kris, Susie, Noelle, Berdly, and various other characters.

SCHOOL

The School is situated inside Hometown, where the adventures of the three heroes began. Alphys and Toriel work here as teachers.

Castle Town

Castle Town is a kingdom led by the Prince of Darkness Ralsei situated in the school closet Dark World. It is where recruited enemies and bosses alike reside.

FIELD OF HOPES AND DREAMS

The Field of Hopes and Dreams is the first area to be seen after the Castle Town's gates have been opened. It is situated in the unused classroom Dark World.

CARD KINGDOM

Card Kingdom is, well, a kingdom led by the tyrant King located in the unused classroom Dark World. This is also where the secret boss Jevil can be found.

CHAPTER 2

CYBER WORLD

CYBER WORLD is the first area to be found in Chapter 2, which is set at the Computer Lab Dark World. It consists of a vast green road with an incredible view up top. At the end of the crossroads, Berdly will fight you.

CYBER CITY

CYBER CITY is, well, a city which can be found after traversing the bottom of the CYBER WORLD. Taking a certain route here with the new party member completely changes the ending.

QUEEN'S MANSION

Queen's Mansion is a massive palace located at the end of CYBER CITY. Three major boss fights occur here, including the secret-slash-final boss, Spamton NEO.

CHAPTER 3

TV WORLD

The TV WORLD is the first area to be found in Chapter 3, which is set in the Dreemurr household Dark World. It welcomes you with a large crowd and even a red carpet.

THE BOARDS

The Boards are a series of games which Tenna forces to make the party play. The game is ironically titled, "The Legend of Tenna". Additionally, a secret game titled "Shadow Mantle" can be found when the S+ rank is achieved in a minigame, which leads to the first secret boss. Upon defeating the first secret boss, the item Shadow Mantle is acquired, which can significantly help in the second secret boss.

GREEN ROOM

The Green Room is a resting area in between Tenna's games.

THE SNOWY ROOM

Though not a full-fledged area, the snowy room is a significant area, as two boss fights occur here, which include Tenna and the second secret (and surprise) boss, The...

CHAPTER 4

THE HOLIDAY RESIDENCE

The Holiday Residence is, well, the Holiday Family's house situated on the left side of Hometown. It is the first new area you visit after sealing Chapter 3's dark fountain.

THE CHURCH

The Church is, well, Hometown's local church. It is where the next dark fountains are located.

THE FIRST SANCTUARY

The First Sanctuary is the first area to be found when traversing the Church Dark World. Here, the party will meet a familiar certain someone which is a returning character from UNDERTALE.

THE SECOND SANCTUARY

The Second Sanctuary is the second area created by Susie's Dark World inside the church. It is mostly messed up.

THE THIRD SANCTUARY

The Third Sanctuary is the third area situated at the church's worshipping area, which holds the main Dark Fountain. It also withholds information about the prophecy Ralsei never told them before.
