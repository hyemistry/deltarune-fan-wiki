CHAPTER 1

The Beginning

THE MYSTERIOUS VOICE

The voice will first ask the player to create their own avatar... or will they?

The choices you made don't matter.

Regardless of what you made, your name will still be...

Your name is...

Kris.

You, the player's soul, now inhabits the body of Kris Dreemurr, the sole human living in the town of Hometown. You then discover how familiar certain characters were...

Are you in an alternate dimension of some sort?

CHALK-EATING MONSTER

After a while of your mother Toriel driving you to the school (despite it being literally near your house), you meet again with your classmates.

Being the latecomer you are, Alphys, your teacher, tells you to get a project partner. To no avail, no one was available except Noelle who was willing to give you a spot in her and Berdly's truce.

Then, she arrived.

Susie. The chalk-eating monster bully.

As Alphys ran out of chalk to write with, she asked you and Susie to go fetch some chalk for her in the school closet... so you did.

Until... you notice something weird: The school closet was shrouded in darkness.

You and Susie assumed it would be fine to enter because it's the school closet anyways. Nothing bad will happen, right?

RIGHT?

WELCOME TO THE UNDERGROUND

nO SORRY That title is from a different story.

Anyway.

You woke up on a desolate land below. Of course it would be dark, too. And you got new clothes! Pretty cool, huh?

But when you arrived, Susie wasn't there. You later found her in the far cliffs, as she had already left before you could even make sense of what's happening.

SPADES!

You and Susie later arrived near a cliff. However, a certain spade person began attacking you!

RUN!

THE PROPHECY

After a while of running away from the weirdo, you two then stumbled upon a... castle?

There was an inhabitant, who introduced himself as Ralsei, the Prince of Darkness. He then tells you about the prophecy of the three heroes.

Susie thought what he was saying was a joke, and so she left almost immediately after.

THE FIELD

And of course, as a good person, you immediately sought after her in the Fields on the east of the kingdom.

SUSIE, STOP!

As she went on a violent spree, now you had to stop here before things go haywire. Since she won't cooperate, you had to manually warn Darkners of her incoming attack!

SUSIE, I SAID STOP!

Susie wouldn't stop—she even joined Lancer!

POOR LANCER

Later on, as the party was captured by the forces of the Card Kingdom, Susie finds a way out of her cell only to discover that Lancer was a part of the scheme after all.

So, she fought him.

But even she couldn't bring herself to hurt him.

So eventually, she stopped being violent for once.

WHO ART THOU?

As the reconciled party explore the rest of the Card Kingdom to find the astray Dark Fountain, a guyeth bearing the glorious nameth of Rouxls Kaard hath shown himself.

HI, LANCER'S DAD!

One boss fight later, they finally encounter the final obstacle in this Dark World: King, Lancer's dad. He's also a spade, so that also makes him the King of Spades.

Enough of my nonsense talk.

Let's go seal that fountain.

DARK FOUNTAIN SEALED

Now that the first Dark Fountain is sealed, what awaits them tomorrow?
